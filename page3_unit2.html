The Basics

Lets start some basics knowledge about the cards in Magic: The Gathering. Firstly magic cards are represented by 5 colours: White, Blue, Black, Red, and Green. Each of these colours tend to follow a theme or style of gameplay.

What are the phases of a turn?

  1. Untap
  2. Upkeep
  3. Draw
  4. Main Phase 1
  5. Combat Phase
  6. Main Phase 2
  7. End Step

Note: Don't worry about the upkeep for now, we'll get to that later.
